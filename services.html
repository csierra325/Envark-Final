What We Offer

Through consultation, collaborative development, and training, I assist in the design and implementation of programs that intend to motivate change.


The most effective way to re-design programs is to assess current practices to identify strengths, weaknesses, and where efforts should be directed. Once this information has been collected and analyzed, offering targeted training through workshops for your staff will build capacity. This allows your staff to implement changes with high efficacy and bridges the design-implementation gap that often exists in these settings. Finally, designing programs that incorporate the findings of the assessment, as well as theoretical and applied behavioral models, will greatly enhance the success of your programs. This success will be measurable and will be a value-add to your institution – donors and funders want to see how their support impacts the community & wildlife. I offer all three of these services as a package (recommended) or individually to meet your needs. Please Contact Me to learn more.
