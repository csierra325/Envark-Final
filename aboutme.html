About Me

Hi, I’m Nicolette and I am so glad you are here.

I am passionate about assisting zoos & aquariums in fulfilling their missions to protect wildlife and wild places by leveraging their reach through conservation programs that target behavior. I combine my deep understanding of behavioral sciences, animal ambassador training & care, and conservation issues to create highly effective programming.

My strengths lie in understanding behavior and creative problem solving – two skills I believe are best used to facilitate the adoption of environmentally beneficial actions.

My background working with animals in zoos & wildlife education facilities prompted me to study how we can utilize this resource to maximize the benefits to our environment. The role of zoos and aquariums has shifted considerably over the past 50 years and championing conservation has become the main focus of many organizations. So, how do we do that effectively? How can we leverage the reach of zoos & aquariums (700 million annual visitors globally) to motivate real and lasting behavior change? How do we ensure that our animal ambassadors’ plight is heard & taken to heart?

My research began to unpack these complexities and has served as the foundation for my services today. If you would like to learn more about me, click here for my full CV or ask me a question here .
